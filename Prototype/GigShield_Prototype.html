1
Sensor polls every 5 minutes
OpenWeatherMap, CPCB AQI, HERE Traffic, and Govt Alert APIs queried for every registered dark-store delivery zone.

Rainfall > 50mm/hr, AQI > 400, Heat > 45°C, Zone speed < 5km/hr — any breach emits a DisruptionEvent for that zone.

Payout = disrupted hours × hourly rate × payout%. Zero manual filing. Zero app interaction from the rider.

Mock UPI transfer fired. Rider push notification sent. Detection to payout: under 30 seconds. No paperwork. Ever.
